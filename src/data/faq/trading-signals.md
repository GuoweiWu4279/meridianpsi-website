question: "Does Meridian provide trading signals or tell me when to buy or sell?"
category: trading
order: 1
---

No. Meridian is a **complete risk manager** — it does everything a traditional risk tool does (daily loss limits, P&L and drawdown caps, single-trade loss caps, loss-streak cutoffs) plus a behavioral monitoring and enforcement layer. It does not provide trading signals, market analysis, entry/exit recommendations, or any form of financial advice.

The stability index reflects patterns in your own behavioral inputs during a session. How you interpret and act on that information is entirely your decision. Please read our [Risk Disclaimer](/disclaimer) before use.

To understand what Meridian actually measures, see the [Features page](/features#seven-dimensions).
