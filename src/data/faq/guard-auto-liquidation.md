---
question: "Does Meridian Guard automatically close my open positions?"
category: trading
order: 2
---

No. Meridian Guard will **never** manage or liquidate your open positions. It is designed to block new entries and enforce discipline.

If Guard triggers a platform lockout (Level 5), you can optionally configure Guard to automatically close open positions, or you can choose to **manually** manage them through your broker.

See the full list of [Guard trigger conditions and response levels](/guard#guard-system).
