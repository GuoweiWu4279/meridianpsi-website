---
question: "What is the difference between Meridian Core and Meridian Guard?"
category: general
order: 1
---

**Meridian Core** ($24.99/mo) provides real-time psychological monitoring, the seven behavioral signal bars, and local session history.

**Meridian Guard** ($34.99/mo) includes everything in Core, plus the automated enforcement system (commitment device) and the advanced Intel Layer analytics.
