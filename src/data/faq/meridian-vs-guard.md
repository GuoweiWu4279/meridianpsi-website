question: "What is the difference between Meridian Core and Meridian Guard?"
category: general
order: 1
---

**[Meridian Core](/pricing)** ($49.99/mo) provides **Meridian PSI** — the real-time behavioral monitor that scores your discipline across seven behavioral signals — plus session history and the full **Intel analytics workspace** (monthly digest, PSI × P&L, weekday patterns, breakdowns, pre-session Risk Brief).

**[Meridian Guard](/guard)** ($69.99/mo) includes everything in Core, plus **Meridian Guard**, the automated enforcement layer (commitment device): rules with six triggers and five response levels, Trading Pause and Disconnect that survive a restart, and **Strict Lock**.

See the [pricing page](/pricing) for a full feature comparison.
