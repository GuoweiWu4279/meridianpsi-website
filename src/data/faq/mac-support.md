---
question: "Is Meridian available for Mac?"
category: technical
order: 3
---

No. Every Meridian app is Windows-only: the native add-on runs inside **NinjaTrader 8** (itself Windows-only), and the standalone apps for **Tradovate** and **Ironbeam** accounts (early access) are Windows applications as well. There is no Mac version. See [Platforms](/platforms) for the full matrix.
