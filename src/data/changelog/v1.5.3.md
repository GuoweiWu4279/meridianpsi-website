Until now your end-of-session journal came out in our format. v1.5.3 hands that to you: arrange it as a set of blocks — turn sections on or off, drag to reorder, rename them, or add your own — by direct manipulation, with a live preview and no template syntax to learn. This build also makes the refreshed session debrief and patterns views the default for everyone. Your profile, Guard rules, journal entries, and history carry over automatically; there is nothing to migrate.

### Your journal, your layout

Open **Journal → Customize** and your journal shows up as blocks: session stats, the timeline, your reflection prompts, and anything you add. Turn each on or off, drag to reorder, rename it, or hit **Add a section** for your own. A live preview shows exactly how the compiled journal will read as you arrange it — what you see is what you get, with nothing to learn. Renaming a prompt now flows through to the journal itself, and your saved notes and answers are never touched — only the layout changes.

### A cleaner debrief and patterns view

The redesigned post-session **debrief** — your honest "what actually happened" — and the **patterns / history** view are now the default for everyone, not just a preview. Same engine underneath; a clearer, calmer surface on top.

### Who should upgrade

Everyone on 1.5.x — especially if you journal your sessions. Your profile, Guard rules, journal, and history are preserved automatically.

### How to upgrade

1. In NinjaTrader 8: **Tools > Remove NinjaScript Assembly**, select **MeridianPSI**, and click **Remove** (your settings and data are kept).
2. **Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On**, and select `MeridianPSI_V1.5.3.zip`.
3. Let NinjaTrader compile and restart if prompted. Your profile, Guard rules, and history carry over automatically.
