### New Features

#### Session Reflection Journal
The Journal page now includes a persistent **Session Reflection** panel with three structured fields — **What went well**, **What to improve**, and **Focus for next session**. Entries are saved per trading day. When you click "Complete Journal," your saved reflection is automatically woven into the compiled journal draft so nothing gets lost between the end of the session and your post-trade review.

#### Warning PSI Zone
A new **Warning** zone now sits between Caution and Critical (PSI 55–71). The alert system fires a distinct "Composure Warning" notification when you first drop into this tier — giving you an earlier, softer prompt before the Critical threshold.

#### Session Window On/Off Toggle
If you trade instruments without fixed session times (24-hour futures, algo strategies), you can now switch off trading-hour monitoring entirely in Settings. When the toggle is off, no alerts fire for out-of-hours activity and the PSI engine skips the session-window dimension.

#### Refresh Accounts Button
A **↻ Refresh accounts** link in the Settings account panel re-reads all connected NT8 accounts on demand — without closing the form or losing changes in progress.

#### Resizable HUD Panels
The HUD overlay now has drag handles between the chart, D-bar signals, and Journal sections. Resize them to fit your screen. The layout is saved and restored across sessions.

### Bug Fixes

- **Journal content lost after navigation** — Compiled journal entries with multiple lines were silently truncated to the first line when navigating away and returning. Root cause was a CSV encoding issue in the day-notes file; fixed with proper newline escaping.
- **Caution zone bar was red** — The Caution row in the Composure Score breakdown was displayed in red because it used the fill-percentage to pick colour rather than the zone identity. Now always yellow.
- **PSI timeline zone bands misaligned** — Zone band proportions did not match the actual PSI thresholds. Corrected: Stable 12%, Caution 33%, Critical 55%.
- **Duplicate section in Session Report** — "Time in State" was shown twice. Removed.
- **Account list always stale** — The Settings account panel was populated once at launch and never refreshed. Accounts connected later were invisible.
- **HUD window width clamped to 860 px** — Saved narrow widths were overridden on restore. Fixed; any width ≥ 400 px is honoured.

### UX Improvements

- **Plain-English alerts** — All HUD banners, toast notifications, and voice alerts have been rewritten. References to D1–D7 codes, "P(t)", and engine internals have been removed from every user-facing string.
- **Settings copy rewrite** — Every card header, field label, and hint in the Settings page now uses plain trader vocabulary. No internal config keys or engine jargon remain visible.
- **Response Level presets** — Advanced settings presets renamed from CONSERVATIVE / BALANCED / AGGRESSIVE to **SENSITIVE / BALANCED / RELAXED**, with a plain-English subtitle for each.
- **Journal mood chips unified** — HUD Quick Journal and Dashboard Journal now both show the same three chips: 😊 Great / 😐 Neutral / 😤 Frustrated. The previous HUD set had duplicate mappings.
