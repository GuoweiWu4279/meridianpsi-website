Patch release. Strongly recommended for everyone, and **required** if you connect to NinjaTrader through a prop firm login (Apex, Topstep, MyFundedFutures, TopstepX, FundedNext, Earn2Trade, and similar).

No profile, guard.xml, or session data migration required.

### What changed

**Activation no longer goes through NinjaTrader's vendor license check.**

Earlier builds asked NinjaTrader to confirm your account was on our vendor registry before the add-on would load. That works for traders signed in with a personal NinjaTrader email account, but it breaks for prop firm traders — who typically don't have a personal NT email at all, since their connection credentials come from the firm. The result was a "Vendor Notification: A license is required" popup at every NinjaTrader start, and Meridian Dashboard never appearing under Tools.

Meridian now activates **purely against your Whop license key**, the same key you copy from your Whop dashboard. This works identically whether you connect to NinjaTrader through your own personal account, through a prop firm, or through multiple firms at once.

### Who should upgrade

- **Prop firm traders:** required. Earlier builds cannot open Meridian Dashboard for you.
- **Personal NinjaTrader account holders:** recommended. Activation is faster and the vendor-notification popup is gone, but v1.3.1 will continue to work for you.

### Upgrade

1. Tools → Import → NinjaScript Add-On → select `MeridianPSI_V1.3.2.zip`.
2. Restart NinjaTrader.
3. Control Center → New → Add-on → Meridian Dashboard.
4. License tab → paste your Whop key → Activate. Existing activations carry over automatically.
