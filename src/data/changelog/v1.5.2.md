A small follow-up to v1.5.1. The main fix: if you blow through your declared Max Daily Loss and then stop for the day, your PSI now reflects it immediately — it no longer waited for a next trade that never came. Your profile, Guard rules, and history carry over automatically; there is nothing to migrate.

### Fixed: a blown Max Daily Loss now registers immediately — even if you stop

If you set a **Max Daily Loss** and then crossed it, Meridian only registered the breach on your *next* trade. So if you stopped out past your declared limit and just sat — the moment that matters most, when you should be done for the day — your PSI didn't move. Now the daily-loss hit lands the instant your realised session P&L crosses your declared stop, with no new entry required, and it re-arms if you recover back above the limit. This only applies if you have actually set a Max Daily Loss in Settings — Meridian measures against the limit you chose, not an absolute number.

### Also in this build

Stability hardening carried over since 1.5.1: the grace window that suppresses the false "no stop loss" alert on reversals is now thread-safe, the session-window check handles overnight hours correctly, and a journal save that fails now tells you instead of failing silently.

### Who should upgrade

Everyone on 1.5.x — especially if you trade against a declared daily-loss limit. Your profile, Guard rules, and history are preserved automatically.

### How to upgrade

1. In NinjaTrader 8: **Tools > Remove NinjaScript Assembly**, select **MeridianPSI**, and click **Remove** (your settings and data are kept).
2. **Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On**, and select `MeridianPSI_V1.5.2.zip`.
3. Let NinjaTrader compile and restart if prompted. Your profile, Guard rules, and history carry over automatically.
