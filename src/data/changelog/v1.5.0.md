This is the largest Meridian release so far. The entire dashboard has been rebuilt around one calm, consistent design system, the Intelligence tab has grown into a full analytics workspace, and Guard gains a real commitment device. Your existing profile, Guard rules, and session history all carry over — there is nothing to migrate.

### A completely redesigned dashboard

Every screen in Meridian has been redrawn from the ground up on a single emerald-on-dark design system, so the whole product now shares the same typography, spacing, color, and motion. It is calmer to read at a glance during a live session and much easier to move around in.

**Overview** is your new pre-session home. The moment you open Meridian it shows you where you stand: your composure trajectory, the one lesson worth carrying over from your last session, the leak most likely to cost you today, the protections you have armed, and a short start-of-session ritual. You get oriented in seconds instead of hunting through tabs.

**Patterns** turns your history into real charts. A month-over-month composure trend, a color-coded session calendar, a weekday breakdown, and a discipline-versus-outcome map let you watch your edge develop over time rather than just react to today.

**Guard** now puts the full response ladder and the new Strict Lock front and center, with a cleaner rule editor and clearer at-a-glance status for every rule you have set.

The **Journal**, **Profile**, **Settings**, **Session Report**, and the floating **HUD** have all moved onto the same system, with smoother transitions and clearer status throughout. The Journal is now customizable — reorder or hide the parts of the daily template you do not use.

### Intelligence, rebuilt

The Intelligence tab is now a proper analytics workspace instead of a single summary screen. Pick any date range — not just preset windows — and break your results down by time of day, hold time, instrument, and long versus short. A dedicated Stats view shows your equity curve, win rate, profit factor, expectancy, and risk metrics alongside the PSI × P&L correlation that puts a dollar figure on staying composed. For the first time you can see exactly where your edge is and where your money leaks.

### Guard Strict Lock

Strict Lock is an optional commitment mode for the times you already know the tilted version of you will want to switch Guard off. Arm it for a fixed window with a cooling-off period and no mid-session bypass: once it is on, you cannot talk yourself out of it until the window ends. It is a genuine commitment device, not a setting you can flip back the moment things get uncomfortable.

### A more reliable auto-flatten

When you have enabled auto-flatten on a Trading Pause or Disconnect rule, Guard now confirms your position is actually closed and retries or escalates if it is not, rather than assuming the first attempt worked. When it matters most, the flatten is verified.

### Fixes

The Overview shortcuts — trajectory, Open Intel, and the rest — now open the right places, and Intelligence reliably shows the data for the exact period you selected (an earlier bug could leave a window empty or show the wrong range).

### Your data, your control

Meridian contributes anonymized trading, behavioral, and feature-usage data to product research, linked only to a random identifier — never your name, account number, credentials, or balances (see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) and [Data &amp; Research Notice](/data-research)). You can opt out at any time; just email us.

### Who should upgrade

Everyone. This release is recommended for all users, and your existing profile, Guard rules, and history are preserved automatically.

### How to upgrade

1. In NinjaTrader 8: Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On, and select `MeridianPSI_V1.5.0.zip`.
2. Restart NinjaTrader if prompted.
3. Your profile, Guard rules, and history carry over automatically.
