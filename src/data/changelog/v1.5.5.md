This release is about Guard doing exactly what you told it to — the moment you cross a line, not a second sooner or later. Several fixes came straight from a live trader whose pause never let go. It also adds a physical hard ceiling on size and order type, and per-account stats for multi-account traders. **Closing or reducing a position is never blocked by anything below — Guard only ever stops you opening new risk.**

### Pauses that actually release

A timed Guard pause is now a clean one-shot. When the minutes are up it releases — even if whatever triggered it is still true. Before, a pause could silently **re-lock itself** the instant it expired (the trigger is often still true while you sit out), turning the timer into a repeat interval; one early user set a ~30-minute pause and got locked out for over an hour. Now a pause fires once per episode, serves its full length, and re-arms only when the condition genuinely clears and comes back.

*Want to stay out until the close instead of a fixed breather? Set the duration to 0 (rest of day, to 5 PM ET).*

### Auto-reconnect after a Disconnect pause

When a timed Disconnect-mode pause ends, Meridian reconnects the exact broker connection it cut — automatically. You (and any order tools that share NinjaTrader's connection) are back online with no manual reconnect. (Rest-of-day Disconnect pauses still stay down until the 5 PM ET close — that's the point of them.)

### Guard acts immediately, and won't double-fire at the line

The old per-rule "grace / wait before activating" delay is gone — every rule fires the instant its condition is met (the default used to be 90 seconds, which let you keep trading after you'd already blown a limit). And a rule now fires once per episode instead of every time a value flickers across its threshold, so PSI hovering at the line gives you one alert, not a stream. The per-rule Cooldown setting is retired — that noise-buffering is now built in.

### New: Hard limits — a physical cap on size and order type (Guard tier)

A new **Hard limits** card on the Guard page lets you set, when calm, a ceiling Guard physically enforces when you're not:

- **Max contracts** — a hard size ceiling. A resting order that would breach it is cancelled; a market order that fills over it is trimmed straight back to the cap (never flipped, never below it).
- **Block entry order types** — forbid specific entry types (Market / Limit / Stop market / Stop limit / MIT). Blocked resting entries are cancelled on arrival.

Exits and reductions are never blocked or trimmed — only new risk over your line. This is separate from your "normal size" setting (which only feeds PSI). When it trims a slipped market fill, Meridian first cancels your own working bracket on that instrument, so it won't orphan a stop/target or trigger NinjaTrader's order-reject pop-ups.

### New: see your numbers per account

On **Intel, Patterns and the Debrief**, a click-to-expand account picker splits your stats, equity curve, breakdowns and per-trade detail by account — so a multi-account trader can see "this account made X, that one Y." Your PSI, composure and Guard are not split — those are yours, not the account's. New feature, so it applies going forward: trades recorded before this update aren't account-tagged and stay under "All accounts." Trade an account once and it appears in the picker automatically.

### Fixed

- **Guard no longer drops out on its own.** Guard features could show as locked ("Guard Tier Required") even for an active Guard subscriber — most often right after updating — until you re-entered your key. Your tier is now resolved by your product/subscription rather than a fixed list of plan IDs, so promo, annual and legacy Guard plans all unlock correctly and stay unlocked across restarts.
- **Settings opens reliably.** Fixed a crash that could close the dashboard when you opened Settings, and improved the contrast on the Display & privacy tab.
- **Acknowledge now blocks new entries by default.** An Acknowledge rule stops new entry orders until you've acknowledged it (still toggleable per rule).
- **A market order that slips past a pause gets trimmed back** to where you were when the pause started — your existing position is never touched or flipped.
- **Loss amounts: just type a positive number** — a stray minus no longer trips a loss rule early.
- **Tidier alerts** — one clear message per event instead of a HUD banner and a corner pop-up saying the same thing; HUD banners no longer overlap; trim and status messages now name the numbers ("Trimmed 2 contracts back to your 3 limit").

### Who should upgrade

Everyone on 1.5.x. Your profile, Guard rules, journal, and history are preserved automatically.

### How to upgrade

1. In NinjaTrader 8: **Tools > Remove NinjaScript Assembly**, select **MeridianPSI**, and click **Remove** (your settings and data are kept).
2. **Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On**, and select `MeridianPSI_V1.5.5.zip`.
3. Let NinjaTrader compile and restart if prompted. Your profile, Guard rules, journal, and history carry over automatically.
