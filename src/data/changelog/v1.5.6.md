This release does two things. It makes Guard read a losing streak the way you do — every loss counts, not just the first — and it makes the monitor tell you *why* your score is moving, in plain words instead of a silent bar. It also fixes two reading errors that mostly bit multi-account setups. **Nothing here ever blocks you closing or reducing a position — Guard only stops you opening new risk.**

### A "losses in a row" pause now fires on every loss, not just the first

If you run a rule like *pause after 3 losses in a row*, it used to fire once — on the loss that first crossed the line — and then go quiet for the rest of the streak. Because a losing streak only ever climbs (nothing resets it until you win), the pause never re-armed on an all-red day, so it felt like "sometimes it works, sometimes it doesn't" — a day with a win in the middle reset the count and made it fire again, an all-loss day fired exactly once.

Now every further loss that keeps the streak going re-fires the pause. A streak that's just holding steady stays quiet (no permanent lock-out), and your cooldown setting still spaces out repeats.

**And a break-even scratch no longer resets your streak.** A tiny in-and-out for zero used to wipe a real losing streak; now only a genuine winner resets it.

### The monitor tells you why — in plain words

The expanded HUD used to show your behavioral signals as bars with a name and a number, but no reason. Now a single line names the **one thing** driving your score right now — "Overtrading," "Oversizing" — the moment it starts to build, and stays silent when nothing is material. Where it's unambiguous it shows the specifics (for example, how many contracts you're holding versus your own max). Hover the line for the full explanation.

The spoken alert was reworded to match: it now names the cause ("PSI dropping. Overtrading.") instead of a vague "check your behavior" — while a genuine emergency still tells you plainly to stop.

### Turn spoken alerts on or off

A new **Spoken alerts** switch in Settings lets you mute the voice nudges (they were previously always on). They stay on by default — the audio cue the moment you start to tilt is doing real work — but it's your call now.

### Fixed

- **Hold time is accurate while you're in a trade.** On some multi-account and copy-trade setups, your time-in-position could read 0 the whole time a position was open, which blinded the overstay signal. It now tracks correctly from your entry.
- **Unrealized P&L is read at your broker's real mark.** A marking bug could value an open position off a placeholder price — making a long look like a huge loss (and falsely trip an "unrealized P&L below" rule) or a short look like a big gain (so the rule never fired). Guard and the Accounts card now read your broker's true unrealized P&L.

### Who should upgrade

Everyone on 1.5.x — especially anyone running a "losses in a row" pause or trading more than one account. Your profile, Guard rules, journal, and history carry over automatically.

### How to upgrade

Meridian ships as a compiled add-on, so you must remove the old version before importing the new one (otherwise NinjaTrader reports "duplicate" names and quietly keeps running the old build):

1. In NinjaTrader 8: **Tools > Remove NinjaScript Assembly**, select **MeridianPSI**, and click **Remove** (your settings and data are kept).
2. **Restart NinjaTrader.** (A removed assembly isn't fully unloaded until you restart — importing before the restart re-creates the conflict.)
3. **Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On**, and select `MeridianPSI_V1.5.6.zip`.
4. Let NinjaTrader compile and restart if prompted. Your profile, Guard rules, journal, and history carry over automatically.
