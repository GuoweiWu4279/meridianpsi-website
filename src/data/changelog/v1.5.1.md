A focused follow-up to v1.5.0 that fixes a false alert some traders hit when reversing a position, and makes the session-window check opt-in so it never flags trading you never asked it to watch. Your profile, Guard rules, and history carry over automatically — there is nothing to migrate.

### Fixed: a false "no stop loss" alert when reversing a position

When you reverse a position — closing with an order that flips your direction — NinjaTrader briefly cancels the old position's protective stop as it tears down the bracket. For a fraction of a second the flipped position has no covering stop, and Meridian was firing a false **"no stop loss"** alert on every reversal. The short grace window that is meant to absorb that stop-reconfiguration moment is now working again, so the false alert is gone. A position that is genuinely unprotected — no stop at all — still alerts correctly.

### Session window is now opt-in

Meridian no longer flags your trading as "outside your session" unless you have actually set your own hours. Earlier builds shipped a default session of 9:30 AM–12:00 PM with the check switched on, which flagged afternoon trading by mistake. Now the session check stays off until you declare your hours in Settings, the suggested close is 4:00 PM rather than noon, and any existing install still carrying the old noon default is switched off automatically.

### Clearer "no accounts found" guidance

If your account list is empty, Meridian now tells you exactly what to do — connect your broker or data feed in NinjaTrader first (only currently-connected accounts are listed), then use Refresh — instead of pointing you to a button that may not be showing.

### Free trial is now 14 days

The free trial is now 14 days, so you have two full weeks to evaluate Meridian before any charge.

### Who should upgrade

Everyone on 1.5.0 — this fixes the reversal false alert and the session-window default. Your profile, Guard rules, and history are preserved automatically.

### How to upgrade

1. In NinjaTrader 8: **Tools > Remove NinjaScript Assembly**, select **MeridianPSI**, and click **Remove** (your settings and data are kept).
2. **Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On**, and select `MeridianPSI_V1.5.1.zip`.
3. Let NinjaTrader compile and restart if prompted. Your profile, Guard rules, and history carry over automatically.
