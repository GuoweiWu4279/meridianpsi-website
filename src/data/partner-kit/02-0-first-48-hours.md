Everything in this kit is organized by topic so you can find things later. But right now you just want to know what to *do*. Here's the path — most partners get their first piece of content live within two days.

---

### Today (about 30 minutes)

**1. Save your details once.** Open §1 and paste your name + promo code into the auto-fill box. Every template on this page then fills in your code and link automatically — you won't have to do it again.

**2. Activate your free Guard license.** Your key is in the welcome email. Install Meridian (§2 → Install) and paste the key in the Dashboard's License tab. You're going to talk about this product — use it first.

**3. Grab the assets.** Hit **Download all assets** in the sidebar. One zip, all the logos, screenshots, and B-roll, sorted into folders. Done.

---

### Day 1 (about 1 hour)

**4. Run one real session.** Market Replay or live SIM — 15 minutes (§2 → Your First 30 Minutes). Watch the PSI move as you trade. This single session is what makes your content credible: you'll be talking from experience, not reading a spec sheet.

**5. Pick your angle.** Read the one that matches your audience (§3 → Angles by Audience) — prop firm, psychology, or systematic. You don't need all of it. You need the framing your viewers already care about.

---

### Day 2 — ship something (1–2 hours)

**6. Make your first piece.** Start small. A **60-second hook** (§6) or a **7-tweet thread** (§6) is the lowest-effort, highest-return first move — far easier than a full review, and it tells you fast whether your audience responds.

**7. Add the disclosure + your link.** Copy the FTC disclosure (§4) and your affiliate link/code into the description. This is required — and the discount is a genuine perk for your audience, so lead with it.

**8. Publish.** That's it. Your link is live in your Whop panel the moment someone clicks.

---

### After you publish

- **Tell us it's live.** Reply to your welcome email with the link. We'll take a look and flag anything (compliance, accuracy) before it can become a problem — and we may feature it.
- **Watch the first 72 hours.** Comments and DMs are where the questions come. The 15-answer Audience FAQ (§3) has accurate, ready answers — respond fast, it builds your credibility.
- **One good piece beats five rushed ones.** If the first lands, we'll talk about doing more — and about the performance tiers that lift your commission to 35% and 40% (§1 → What You Earn).
