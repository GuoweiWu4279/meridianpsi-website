### Usage guidelines

**Do:**
- Overlay screenshots in your video thumbnails — the PSI HUD is visually distinct and communicates the product instantly
- Use the B-roll video as background footage, intro/outro, or picture-in-picture while you narrate
- Pair the warning/critical state screenshots with your content about discipline breakdowns — these are the most compelling visuals

**Do not:**
- Modify the logo (no color changes, stretching, or effects)
- Use screenshots to imply specific performance outcomes ("my PSI was 95 every day this week")
- Use Meridian branding alongside content that violates the compliance guidelines in §4
