**Tips:**

- The affiliate disclosure must appear *above* the fold вҖ” before the "Show more" cutoff in the YouTube description. Place it before or immediately after the links section, not at the very end
- Keep the timestamps section even if you don't have many вҖ” YouTube's chapter feature improves watch time
- The "NOTE" at the bottom is recommended but not legally required for the description specifically; it reinforces the right framing for your audience
