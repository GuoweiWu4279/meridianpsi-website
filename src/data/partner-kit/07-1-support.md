### Direct contact

**Email:** [garycaffrey@meridian-partnership.com](mailto:garycaffrey@meridian-partnership.com)

**Response time:** Within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent issues (e.g. your affiliate link isn't tracking), we aim for under 4 hours during business hours.

**What to include when you write:**
- Your channel name or the email address you used to apply
- A clear description of what you need — one sentence is enough
- Any relevant link, screenshot, or draft you want feedback on

---

### What we're here for

- **Compliance questions** — "Can I say X in my video?" — ask before publishing, always
- **Technical product questions** — anything the FAQ didn't answer or that came up live in your session
- **Broken affiliate link or promo code** — flag immediately and we'll fix it within hours
- **Asset requests** — custom screenshots, specific HUD states, higher-res versions
- **Audience questions you can't answer** — forward the question and we'll give you the accurate answer

---

### What to expect from the partnership

**Updates:** When Meridian ships a significant new feature or version, we'll email you with a summary and updated kit materials if anything changes. You don't need to monitor the site.

**Kit updates:** This page is a live document — it updates as we improve the product and refine the talking points. The "Last updated" date at the top tells you when something changed. You don't need to re-read it cover to cover; just check the date if you haven't visited in a while.

**New assets:** As we produce new screenshots, B-roll footage, or creative templates, they'll be added to the asset package and you'll be notified.

**Ongoing relationship:** If your content performs well with your audience, we'll reach out proactively about custom arrangements — higher commission tiers, co-created content, or early access to new features for review purposes. There's no fixed path; it develops based on what's actually working.
