**Tips for this format:**

- Pace the first three seconds like a hard cut — no intro, no "hey guys", no context-setting. Open directly on the hook
- Use text overlays throughout — a large portion of short-form viewers watch muted
- The disclosure can be a brief text overlay rather than spoken — this is acceptable for short-form content
- If you show the Meridian HUD on screen, the product explains itself — you don't need to describe it verbally in as much detail
- "Before the damage — not after" is the differentiating claim; deliver it with a beat of emphasis
