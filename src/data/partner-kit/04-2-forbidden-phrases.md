title: What Not to Say
section: compliance
order: 2
---

These aren't arbitrary restrictions. Each one protects you from FTC liability or your audience from a false impression about what Meridian actually does.

---

| Do not say | Why | Say instead |
|---|---|---|
| "Meridian will make you profitable" | False — it monitors behavior, not trade outcomes | "Meridian monitors behavioral patterns that often precede losses" |
| "Guaranteed to improve your trading" | Guarantees are a compliance red line | "Designed to support better execution discipline" |
| "Eliminates emotions" / "no more emotional trading" | Impossible claim — Meridian creates friction, not emotional elimination | "Helps monitor behavioral pressure as it builds" |
| "Prevents blowups" | Overclaims outcomes Meridian cannot guarantee | "Intervenes when behavioral signals indicate elevated risk" |
| "Reduces losses" | Financial outcome claim — not provable per-user | "Intervenes during behavioral patterns associated with loss spirals" |
| "This will make you a better trader" | Outcome claim | "Gives you real-time visibility into your behavioral patterns during the session" |
| "Proven trading results" | Makes an evidential claim that doesn't exist at a per-user level | "Used by active NinjaTrader 8 traders who want behavioral feedback" |
| "100% accurate" | Nothing in trading is 100% accurate | Remove — no qualifier makes this acceptable |
| "Risk-free" | The trial requires a credit card and trading itself is not risk-free | "7-day free trial — cancel anytime during the trial period" |
| "Works on all platforms" | NT8 + Windows only | "Built exclusively for NinjaTrader 8 on Windows" |

---

**The underlying principle:** Meridian is a behavioral monitoring tool. It monitors patterns and can intervene. What happens after the intervention — whether the trader improves, whether outcomes change — is outside the product's scope to claim.

Keep claims inside the product's actual capability boundary and you'll be fine.
