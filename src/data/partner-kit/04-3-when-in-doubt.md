If you're unsure whether a specific claim, framing, or piece of content is within the right parameters, ask before publishing.

**Contact:** [garycaffrey@meridian-partnership.com](mailto:garycaffrey@meridian-partnership.com)

**Response time:** Within 24 hours on weekdays, typically faster.

**What to send:** A draft of the specific section you're unsure about — one or two sentences, or the full script if it's faster. No need to write a detailed explanation; just flag what you're uncertain about and we'll give you a direct answer.

---

**Common situations where it's worth asking:**

- You want to describe a personal trading experience with Meridian and aren't sure how to frame it
- A viewer asked you a technical question in a comment and you're not 100% sure of the answer
- You want to make a comparison claim (e.g. "better than X")
- You're writing something that touches on financial outcomes ("since using Meridian, my P&L has...")
- You want to cover a feature you haven't seen documented anywhere

We'd rather you ask and publish confidently than publish and have to correct something later.
