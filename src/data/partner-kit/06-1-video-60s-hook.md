**Tips for this format:**

- The hook works best when you pause after "I knew that was coming" — let it land before continuing
- The PSI concept lands better when you show it on screen simultaneously; use a screenshot or B-roll from the asset pack
- Swap "[X weeks/months]" for your actual experience — even "I've been testing it" is fine during your trial period
- This script is designed for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok — keep the pacing tight, no dead air
- Record the CTA in your normal voice, not a scripted one — your audience notices the tonal shift
